03 / NOT DOWNLOADED

External pages stay external.

User action is required

Operator pages and files open only after the rider taps a link. JustGo does not bundle, fetch, preview, cache, prefetch, or redistribute them, and they are never counted as offline content.

  • The directory’s 330 records comprise 275 Maps, 28 Travel, 6 Accessibility, and 21 Help links across 275 unique targets.
  • Hong Kong’s external MTR targets are 1 System Map, 98 Location Maps, 98 Station Layouts, and 14 Light Rail Street Maps—not included map files.
  • Attribution, license, and provider links behave the same way: opening them contacts the destination only after a user action.
  • If an external destination changes or disappears, JustGo does not retain a hidden offline copy.

04 / ONLINE-ONLY BEHAVIOR

Live means a provider request.

Hong Kong has live-arrival references for all 162 matched stations. Asking for current arrivals contacts the DATA.GOV.HK government transport API. The returned current values are not part of the offline pack and should not be described as static schedules.

Place search and walking approaches may also require Apple services and connectivity. Included metro network geometry does not make every part of trip planning offline.

05 / PERSONAL MEDIA

Private storage is not a public pack.

Personal station media remains entirely on the rider’s device. It is not downloaded as public JustGo content, uploaded into the included city packs, or used as verified routing, transfer, platform, or door data.

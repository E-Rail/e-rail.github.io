01 / THE IDEA

“A route can be technically possible
and still be practically impossible.”

JustGo builds subway paths from locally bundled network graphs, asks Apple Maps for place search and walking legs, then checks critical stations against source-backed city packs. The result is a route with reasons, caveats, and data confidence—not a mysterious green score.

High confidence · 80–100Fewer concerns in the available data
Worth checking · 60–79One or more details need attention
Plan carefully · 0–59Complex, risky, or data-limited

02 / JULY 2026 BUILD

More than
A to B.

The current app has grown far beyond the original six-city concept.

01

46 local network graphs

Physical-track geometry and station topology are bundled for 46 cities, so subway paths follow the actual network—not a generic straight line.

02

Door-to-door planning

Apple Maps resolves places and walking approaches; JustGo connects them to stations and generates fastest, least-walking, and fewest-transfer alternatives.

03
◎EXPLAINED

Explainable confidence

The 0–100 score accounts for transfers, walking, source coverage, service hours, feasibility, crowd-control windows, and your own local reports.

04
✣

13 richer city packs

Downloadable packs add the facts each source can support: schedules, accessibility, facilities, maps, exits, and crowd-control windows.

05
GO

A hands-on Go companion

Manual step progression, route-map previews, speech, haptics, visual announcements, and clearly labeled time-estimated get-off reminders.

06
LOCAL⌂

Personal, on your device

Favorites, Home and Work tags, saved trips, history, active trips, preferences, and accessibility reports stay in the app’s local storage.

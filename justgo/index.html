01 / THE IDEA

“A route can be technically possible
and still be practically impossible.”

Traditional map apps optimize for time. JustGo looks at the trip as a human will experience it: transfers, walking, confusing stations, accessibility, and how trustworthy the available data actually is.

High confidenceClear route, reliable data
Worth checkingSome details need attention
Plan carefullyComplex or limited information

02 / WHAT IT KNOWS

More than
A to B.

JustGo turns scattered transit details into one understandable journey.

01

Human-centered routes

Choose fastest, least walking, fewest transfers, least confusing, luggage friendly, or step-free.

02

Station intelligence

Entrances, exits, schedules, station maps, facilities, and accessibility details in one place.

03

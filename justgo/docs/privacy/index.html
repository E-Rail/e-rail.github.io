01 / SCOPE & SUMMARY

Privacy follows the data boundary.

This policy covers the JustGo app and the JustGo pages on e-rail.github.io. Third-party services process their own requests under their own policies.

Included data is local app content

The 58-city catalog, 46 route-picker baselines, two offline city packs, and included pilot media do not require opening their source websites during ordinary use.

Personal station media stays on-device

It remains entirely on-device and is never used as verified routing, transfer, platform, or door data.

Station-sign recognition stays on-device

A camera request can occur only after the rider taps Scan Sign. Frames are processed locally and are not saved or uploaded.

Hong Kong live arrivals are online

Viewing current arrivals contacts the DATA.GOV.HK government transport API for the referenced station and line.

Official-resource links require a tap

The 330 directory records are URL metadata. Their 275 unique targets are not bundled, fetched, previewed, cached, prefetched, or redistributed by JustGo.

02 / INCLUDED APP DATA

Reading included files does not call their sources.

The app includes catalog metadata for 58 cities, OpenStreetMap-derived route-picker baselines for 46 cities, 606 station records across the Beijing and Hong Kong offline packs, and two licensed pilot images. Reading these included resources does not require a runtime request to OpenStreetMap, MTR, DATA.GOV.HK, or the image source pages.

Source and license links remain available for transparency. Opening an attribution link is a separate user action and may contact that website.

Hong Kong transit data and live arrivals are attributed to MTR Corporation Limited and DATA.GOV.HK under the DATA.GOV.HK Terms of Use. JustGo does not control provider logging, retention, or service availability.

On-device station-sign recognition does not contact an OCR or image-analysis provider. Camera frames are processed by Apple Vision APIs on the device and are not retained by JustGo.

05 / CAMERA & MEDIA INPUT

A scan starts only when the rider asks.

  • Opening Live Go or reaching a transfer does not request camera access. The request can occur only after the rider taps Scan Sign in verified indoor guidance.
  • Recognized sign text is matched on-device against authored checkpoint aliases. Frames are not saved or uploaded.
  • VoiceOver uses manual checkpoint confirmation before the camera starts. Denied or unavailable camera access also falls back to manual controls.
  • Current included packs contain zero verified transfer contexts, so the scanner code does not create current indoor-routing coverage.
  • Personal station media is chosen through the system Photos picker or file importer. JustGo redraws it into a metadata-free JPEG, stores only the app copy, and excludes that storage directory from backup.

06 / EXTERNAL LINKS

Nothing opens until the rider asks.

Round 35’s official-resource directory contains 330 hyperlink records—275 Maps, 28 Travel, 6 Accessibility, and 21 Help—across 275 unique targets.

  • Operator pages and files open only after the rider taps a link.
  • JustGo does not bundle, fetch, preview, cache, prefetch, or redistribute those destinations.
  • The destinations are not counted as included or offline content.
  • Hong Kong’s map targets are 1 MTR System Map, 98 Location Maps, 98 Station Layouts, and 14 Light Rail Street Maps; each remains external.
  • After a rider taps a link, the browser or system web view connects to the destination. The provider may receive the requested URL, IP address, request time, browser information, and any data the rider chooses to submit there.
  • The destination’s privacy policy and terms govern that visit.

07 / THIS WEBSITE

One preference is remembered.

The language switcher stores the selected display language as erail-language in browser local storage and as the first-party erail_language cookie. The cookie is limited to the site path, uses SameSite=Lax, and is marked Secure when the page is served over HTTPS. If no choice is saved, the page uses the browser’s preferred language.

The site is hosted with GitHub Pages and loads font resources from Google Fonts. Those providers may receive ordinary web-request metadata under their own policies. The JustGo pages do not use the language preference to build a transit profile.

08 / RETENTION & DELETION

Controls live where the record lives.

  • Delete saved trips, trip records, Station Quick Tags, personal station media, and related records through their app controls where available.
  • Delete the app and its data to remove the app container, subject to iOS backup and restore settings.
  • Revoke location, camera, or notification permission in iOS Settings to stop future use of those permissions.
  • A live-arrival response is provider data; provider-side request logs are controlled by DATA.GOV.HK and its infrastructure, not by JustGo.
  • Clear this site’s cookie and local storage in browser settings to remove the saved language preference.

09 / SECURITY & CHILDREN

Device protections still matter.

On-device records rely on the normal iOS app sandbox and device protections. Network requests use the operating system’s networking facilities. No method can eliminate every risk, especially on a compromised device, browser, or network.

JustGo is a general transit utility. It does not ask a rider to create a public JustGo profile for the features described in this policy. Third-party services may apply their own age requirements and privacy rules.

10 / CHANGES & CONTACT

A changed data flow requires a changed policy.

If JustGo adds account sync, uploads, analytics, advertising, another live provider, or a different use of personal media, this policy will be revised and the effective date will change.

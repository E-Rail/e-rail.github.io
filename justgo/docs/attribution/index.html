02 / HONG KONG DATA

MTR Corporation Limited and DATA.GOV.HK.

Hong Kong station data and live arrivals are attributed to MTR Corporation Limited and DATA.GOV.HK under the DATA.GOV.HK Terms of Use. The included pack has 162 matched station records and 98 accessibility records. Live-arrival references cover all 162 matched stations and request current results from the government transport API.

The pack also contains 98 external layout references. Those references do not include or reproduce the destination layouts: each provider page opens only after a user action and remains governed by its own notices and terms.

Linked does not mean included.

02 / HONG KONG DATA

MTR Corporation Limited and DATA.GOV.HK.

Hong Kong station data is reusable under the DATA.GOV.HK government reuse terms. The included pack has 162 station records and 98 accessibility records. Data and live arrivals are attributed to MTR Corporation Limited and DATA.GOV.HK; live-arrival references cover all 162 included stations and request current results from the government transport API.

The separate external catalog points to the official MTR indexes for 1 System Map, 98 Location Maps, 98 Station Layouts, and 14 Light Rail Street Maps. These operator files are hyperlinks only: they are not bundled, fetched, previewed, cached, prefetched, or redistributed by JustGo.

04 / EXTERNAL MATERIAL

Linked does not mean included.

The separate Round 35 directory contains 330 factual hyperlink records: 275 Maps, 28 Travel, 6 Accessibility, and 21 Help links across 275 unique targets. Transit-operator pages and files, source pages, license pages, and other external websites retain their own copyright, database rights, terms, trademarks, and privacy practices.

Every operator destination opens only after the rider taps a link. JustGo does not bundle, fetch, preview, cache, prefetch, or redistribute the destination material, and does not count it as offline content.

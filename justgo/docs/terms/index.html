02 / THE SERVICE

Informational transit tools.

JustGo provides informational tools for city and station discovery, metro route planning where network data exists, track-shaped route previews, map-first trip guidance, walking approaches, route comparison, accessibility context, live Hong Kong arrival lookups, Station Quick Tags, local trip memory, and trip-step cards.

JustGo is an independent open-source project. It is not a transit operator, emergency service, accessibility certification body, or official indoor-navigation system. Attribution to a provider does not imply that provider sponsors, certifies, or endorses JustGo.

04 / SAFETY & ACCURACY

Never treat an estimate as an instruction from staff.

Verify before and during travel

Check official operator notices, station signage, platform displays, announcements, barriers, and staff instructions. In an emergency or evacuation, follow authorities and station personnel—not JustGo.

  • Routes, durations, fares, walking times, service states, live arrivals, accessibility facts, external resources, reminders, and confidence labels may be delayed, incomplete, unavailable, stale, or wrong.
  • An accessibility record can describe a known facility without proving that it works now or that every indoor segment is step-free.
  • Verified indoor transfer coverage is zero. Route-picker geometry and external maps do not prove an indoor path, platform, door, or transfer.
  • Station-sign recognition is a rider-started convenience, not a safety or location guarantee. Confirm the station manually before relying on the result.
  • A high-confidence label is a comparison aid, not a probability, safety certification, accessibility guarantee, or operator instruction.
  • Do not use JustGo as the sole source for last-train decisions, emergency movement, medical transport, or any journey where delay or error could cause serious harm.

01 / SERVICE MODEL

The label determines the behavior.

A station field is not simply “available.” It belongs to one of four classes, and the app must preserve that class from display through network behavior.

APP CONTENTIncluded data

Catalog metadata, 46 route-picker baselines, two offline city packs, and licensed media shipped with the build.

 USER ACTIONOfficial-resource links

330 operator hyperlinks across 275 unique targets that open only when the rider taps them.

 ONLINE REQUESTLive Hong Kong arrivals

Current results requested from the government transport API for referenced stations.

 ON DEVICEPersonal station media

Rider-selected media that stays local and never influences routing or guidance.
No category promotion

An official-resource URL is not an included page or file. A live-arrival reference is not an offline timetable. A personal photo is not route evidence. Missing data remains missing.

02 / INCLUDED DATA

Files available as app content.

Included means the data or media is delivered with the app build and may be counted as offline content. It does not require opening a provider webpage.

03 / EXTERNAL REFERENCES

A link is not a local asset.

The separate directory contains 330 hyperlink records: 275 Maps, 28 Travel, 6 Accessibility, and 21 Help, resolving to 275 unique targets.

  • JustGo opens an operator page or file only after the rider taps its link.
  • JustGo does not bundle, fetch, preview, cache, prefetch, or redistribute those destinations.
  • External pages and files are not counted as offline content.
  • Hong Kong links to 1 MTR System Map, 98 Location Maps, 98 Station Layouts, and 14 Light Rail Street Maps.
  • After opening, the destination provider and the rider’s browser or system web view handle the connection under their own terms and privacy practices.
  • A broken or changed external page does not become an included fallback copy.

04 / LIVE ARRIVALS

Hong Kong requires a live request.

All 162 matched Hong Kong stations have live-arrival references. When the rider asks to view current arrivals, JustGo contacts the DATA.GOV.HK government transport API.

PROVIDERMTR Corporation Limited through DATA.GOV.HK.
TRIGGERA rider opens or refreshes a Hong Kong live-arrival view.
REQUESTThe referenced station and line context plus ordinary network metadata needed for the API connection.
OFFLINE STATUSLive responses are online service output and are not counted as included or offline station data.
STATIC SCHEDULESNone are included for Hong Kong in this snapshot.
TERMSMTR Corporation Limited and DATA.GOV.HK are credited under the DATA.GOV.HK Terms of Use.

05 / PERSONAL MEDIA

Local context stays local.

Personal station media belongs to the rider’s private, on-device layer.

  • Personal station media remains on the device and is not part of the public city packs.
  • JustGo does not upload it as a station-data contribution.
  • It is never used as verified routing, transfer, platform, or door data.
  • Removing the media or deleting the app removes JustGo’s local use of that media, subject to the device’s own backup behavior.

06 / MAPS & ROUTING

Surface routing is not indoor routing.

Place search, device location, and walking approaches may use Apple frameworks and therefore may require a network connection. Route-picker baselines are included for 46 cities; 12 catalog-only cities do not have one.

Current indoor boundary

Verified indoor transfer coverage is zero. Route-picker geometry and external maps do not establish an indoor path, platform, door, transfer, or verified step-free route.
